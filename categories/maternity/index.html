Planning Tip

Ideal Timing for Your Photoshoot

Maternity sessions are commonly planned during the later second trimester or early third trimester, when the baby bump is clearly visible and the mother is generally comfortable enough for the session.

The ideal timing varies by pregnancy, so personal comfort and medical guidance should always take priority. We recommend reserving your date early in your second trimester to secure your preferred slot.

FAQ

Maternity questions

What is the price of a maternity photoshoot in Delhi NCR?

Our maternity packages currently begin at ₹3,499 for one theme and one gown. Packages with additional themes, gowns and a reel are available up to ₹10,499.

Which locations do you serve for maternity photography?

We serve families across Delhi NCR, including Delhi, Noida, Greater Noida, Noida Extension, Gurgaon/Gurugram and Faridabad. Location availability can be confirmed while booking.

Are gowns included in the maternity package?

Yes. The number of gowns depends on the package selected, beginning with one gown in the ₹3,499 package and going up to four gowns in the ₹10,499 package.

Can my husband or family join the maternity shoot?

Couple and family photographs may be included depending on the selected concept, package and session plan. Confirm the participants while booking so the shoot can be planned accordingly.

Do you offer studio and outdoor maternity shoots?

Yes, maternity sessions can be planned in studio or suitable outdoor settings, subject to the package, weather, location availability and applicable additional charges.

When should I book my maternity shoot?

It is advisable to enquire early enough to reserve a suitable date. The exact shoot timing should be based on the mother’s comfort and individual pregnancy.

How many themes and gowns are included?

Packages currently include: ₹3,499: 1 theme and 1 gown; ₹5,499: 2 themes and 2 gowns; ₹7,499: 3 themes and 3 gowns; ₹10,499: 5 themes, 4 gowns and 1 reel.

Is a reel included in every maternity package?

A reel is included in the ₹10,499 package. It can also be selected as an add-on for other packages, subject to availability.

Do you provide maternity albums and frames?

Yes. Albums and frames are available as paid add-ons. Current offer prices should be displayed in the add-ons section.

How can I book a maternity session?

Visitors should be able to enquire using the website contact form, WhatsApp, or the available contact number. When the form is opened from the maternity page, the maternity service must already be selected.
