Frequently Asked Questions

When should I book my baby photoshoot?

We recommend booking at least 2–3 weeks in advance. For newborn sessions, the ideal window is typically within the first 5–15 days after birth, but we can accommodate later bookings as well.

How much does a baby photoshoot cost?

Packages start from around ₹1,599 for themed mini sessions and go up to ₹15,000+ for full collections. Final price depends on session length, number of edited images, and add-ons like albums or prints.

Do you provide outfits and props?

Yes — we provide a selection of newborn wraps, props, and themed outfits. You're welcome to bring your own items too.

Do you offer Janmashtami or other themed shoots?

Yes, seasonal theme shoots (including Janmashtami) are available. Theme sessions are limited and should be booked early.

Do you offer outdoor maternity shoots in Delhi?

Yes — we offer outdoor maternity sessions at parks and scenic locations. Some locations may require permits or a small travel fee.

How long until I receive my edited photos?

Typical turnaround is 2–3 weeks for the full edited gallery. Rush delivery can be arranged for an extra fee.

Do you photograph newborns? What’s the best age?

Yes — ideal time for posed newborn shots is 5–15 days old. Family and lifestyle newborn sessions can be scheduled later as well.

How do I book a session?

You can book via our website booking form, call +91-7042926912, or message us on WhatsApp for fast replies and availability.

Can parents and siblings be included?

Yes — family members are welcome. Please tell us the group size when booking so we can plan the session time and setup.
