Frequently Asked Questions

What should we bring to the photoshoot?

For newborn sessions, bring extra diapers, wipes, and a bottle. For older babies, bring their favorite toys and snacks. We'll provide props and outfits, but feel free to bring any special items you'd like to include.

How long does it take to receive the edited photos?

You can typically expect to receive your fully edited, high-resolution photo gallery within 2-3 weeks after your session date. We take pride in our editing process to make sure every photo is perfect.

Can we include family members in the photoshoot?

Absolutely! Parents and siblings are welcome to be part of the session. Please let us know when booking so we can plan accordingly. There may be a small additional fee for larger family groups.
