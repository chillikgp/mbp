FAQ

Before you book

Where is the My Baby Pictures studio?

The studio is in Saini Enclave, Karkardooma, East Delhi, with sessions available across Delhi NCR.

How is session pricing determined?

Pricing is structured around session type, duration, and setup complexity. We offer distinct packages for maternity, newborn, milestones, cake smashes, festivals, and events.

Do you offer wardrobe and styling options?

Yes! The studio provides a curated collection of maternity gowns, baby wraps, outfits, and themed props for all booked sessions.

Can we include family and sibling photos?

Absolutely. Sibling and parents/family portraits are welcomed and included in our signature and legacy session packages.

How do I book?

Use the inquiry form, call the studio or message on WhatsApp to check availability and reserve a slot.
