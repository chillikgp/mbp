Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 2024

At My Baby Pictures, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information when you book a session or fill out our contact form, including:

  • Name and contact details (Phone/WhatsApp).
  • Baby's age, due date, or milestones for session planning.
  • Photos taken during your session (managed as per your consent).

2. How We Use Your Data

The information provided is used exclusively for:

  • Scheduling and confirming your photography appointments.
  • Providing you with your edited photo gallery.
  • Improving our website experience through Google Analytics.

3. Data Protection

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We implement various security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information when you enter, submit, or access your data.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to understand and save your preferences for future visits and compile aggregate data about site traffic so that we can offer better site experiences in the future.
